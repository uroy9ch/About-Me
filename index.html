About me:

Hey there, welcome to my "About Me" page. I guess the most interesting thing to know about me is that I like mostly things that go fast!

Fast cars, fast boats, or a fast two wheeled vehicle interests me.

I guess the reason why fast stuff interests me is because I'm an Industrial Designer at my core and fast stuff usually have a very dynamic and strong design purpose. I also get a kick out of strong, strategic Design as it applies to Interactive Design and brand awareness. Back in November 2014 I attended a brand experience installation sponsored and featuring Red Bull. They had artists create sculptures of their Red Bull cans. Not selling anything - just providing a brand experience to the general public - whether they were aware of the product or not. Just a smart and strategic approach to a marketing and brand awareness campaign.
